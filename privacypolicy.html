Privacy Policy

At CN Cares, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our mobile repair services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information from you:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, physical address
  • Device Information: Brand, model, serial number, and issues reported
  • Payment Details: Mode of payment (no card or bank data is stored)
  • Service History: Records of past repairs, invoices, and communication
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect your information to:

  • Provide and manage repair services
  • Contact you for updates, confirmations, or delivery arrangements
  • Send you service-related notifications
  • Maintain repair records and warranties
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Respond to queries or complaints
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Protection

We follow industry-standard practices to protect your data:

  • Information is stored securely and accessible only to authorized staff
  • We never store sensitive payment information like card numbers or CVVs
  • Your data is only retained as long as necessary for service or legal purposes

4. Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website may use cookies to collect basic usage information such as:

  • Pages viewed
  • Time spent on the site
  • Device/browser type
This helps us enhance website performance and user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools (such as Google Analytics or payment gateways). These services may collect data as per their own privacy policies, which are independent of CN Cares.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Ask us to delete your personal data (subject to legal or operational requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
